How to Use Gestures

Head Mode

  • Move Cursor: Move your head.
  • Left Click: Open your mouth.

Hand Mode

  • Move Cursor: Point with your index finger.
  • Left Click: Pinch your thumb and index finger together.
  • Right Click: Pinch your thumb and middle finger together.

  • 🖐 Full Palm – Clear board (with confirmation)

  • 📏 Cursor Size – Adjust brush width
  • 🧼 Eraser – Remove small parts
  • 🧹 Clear Board – Erase everything instantly

🗂 File Handling

  • 📤 Upload PDF / PPT / Image – Import study material
  • 📄 Page Navigation – Use ⬅ / ➡ to change pages
